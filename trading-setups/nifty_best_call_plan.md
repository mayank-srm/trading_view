## Best Call Setup

Take a call only if:

1. A 5-minute candle closes above 23,520.
2. Price holds above 23,520 for the next candle.
3. Fast EMA is above slow EMA, or price is holding above VWAP.
4. Volume expands on the breakout candle.

Targets:

- Target 1: 23,600
- Target 2: 23,680
- Stretch target: 23,800

Invalidation:

- Exit/avoid if price closes back below 23,520 after breakout.
- Aggressive stop can be below the breakout candle low.

## Best Put Setup

Take/consider put only if:

1. Price rejects 23,520-23,600 and closes back below 23,520, or
2. A 5-minute candle closes below 23,400.

Targets:

- Target 1: 23,320
- Target 2: 23,280
- Stretch target: 23,200

Invalidation:

- Exit/avoid if price closes back above 23,520.
- Strong put view weakens above 23,600.

Inside this range, avoid directional option entries unless there is a very clean rejection candle at one edge. Wait for breakout or breakdown.
